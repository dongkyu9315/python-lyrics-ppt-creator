385 Conquering Now and Still to Conquer

385 군기를 손에 높이 들고

- 1
Con-quer-ing now and still to con-quer, Rid-eth a King in His might,

군기를 손에 높이 들고 다 빨리 나아가세

-
Lead-ing the host of all the faith-ful In-to the midst of the fight;

진리의 검을 앞세우고 힘차게 싸워보세

-
See them withe cour-age ad-vanc-ing, Clad in their bril-liant ar-ray,

온몸에 갑주 입고서 담대히 나가보세

-
Shout-ing the name of their Lead-er, Hear them ex-ult-ing-ly say:

군기를 들고 나가세 승리는 내 것일세

-
Not to the strong is the bat-tle, Not to the swift is the race,

십자가 군병들아 두 손에 기를 들고

-
Yet to the true and the faith-ful vic-t'ry is prom-ised thro' grace.

용맹스럽게 싸우세 승리는 내 것일세

- 2
Con-quer-ing now and still to con-quer, Who is this won-der-ful King?

천국에 있는 천군천사 우리를 지키리라

-
Whence are the ar-mies which He lead-eth, While of His glo-ry they sing?

하나님께서 내게 주신 튼튼한 갑주입고

-
He is our Lord and Re-deem-er, Sav-ior and Mon-arch di-vine;

주님의 명령지켜서 어디든 다 나가세

-
They are the stars that foe-ev-er Bright in His King-dom will shine.

하늘에 계신 주님이 우리를 맞으시리

-
Not to the strong is the bat-tle, Not to the swift is the race,

십자가 군병들아 두 손에 기를 들고

-
Yet to the true and the faith-ful vic-t'ry is prom-ised thro' grace.

용맹스럽게 싸우세 승리는 내 것일세

- 3
Con-quer-ing now and still to con-quer, Je-sus, Thou Ru-ler of all,

주님의 원수 마귀들이 겁내어 달아날 때

-
Thrones and their scepters all shall per-ish, Crowns and their splen-dor shall fall,

발걸음 빨리 재촉하여 앞으로 속히 나가

-
Yet shall the ar-mies Thou lead-est, Faith-ful and true to the last,

원수를 정복하고서 인생을 구해내세

-
Find in Thy man-sions e-ter-nal Rest, when their warfare is past.

죽음을 두려워말고 앞으로 전진하세

-
