11 Worship the Lord in the Beauty of Holiness

11 거룩한 주님께

- 1
Wor-ship the Lord in the beau-ty of ho-li-ness;

거룩한 주님께 예배를 드리세

-
Bow down be-fore Him, His glo-ry pro-claim;

무릎을 꿇어서 영광 돌려

-
Gold of o-be-dience, and in-cense of low-li-ness

금 같은 복종과 향기론 겸손을

-
Bring and a-dore Him the Lord is His name.

주님께 바쳐서 경배하세

- 2
Low at His feet lay thy bur-den of care-ful-ness;

주 앞에 삼가서 겸손히 행하면

-
High on His heart He will bear it for thee,

주 너를 언제나 돌보시네

-
Com-fort thy sorrows, and answer thy prayerful-ness,

말씀엔 위로와 기도엔 응답을

-
Guid-ing thy steps as may best for thee be.

네 갈길 바르게 인도하네

- 3
Fear not to en-ter His courts, in the slen-der-ness,

주 앞에 설 때에 두려워 말아라

-
Of the poor wealth thou wouldst reck-on as thine;

가난한 우리를 도우시네

-
Truth in its beau-ty and love in its ten-der-ness,

보배론 진리와 온유한 사랑은

-
These are the of-ferings to lay on His shrine.

주님께 다 바칠 귀한 예물

- 4
These, though we bring them in trem-bling and fear-ful-ness,

떨면서 바치는 이 작은 예물도

-
He will ac-cept for the Name that is dear,

주님은 귀하게 받으시네

-
Morn-ings of joy give for even-ings of tear-ful-ness,

어둔 밤 지나고 새 아침 맞으니

-
Trust for our trem-bling, and hope for our fear.

믿음과 소망을 늘 주시네
