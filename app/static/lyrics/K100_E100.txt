100 Chief of Sinners Though I Be

100 죄인 괴수 날 위해

- 1
Chief of sin-ners though I be,

죄인 괴수 날 위해

-
Je-sus shed His blood for me;

십자가를 지신 주

-
Died that I might live on high,

길과 진리 되시며

-
Lived that I might nev-er die.

우리 생명 되시네

-
As the branch is to the vine,

내가 주와 하나됨

-
I am His, and He is mine.

포도 비유 같도다

- 2
Oh, the height of Je-sus' love!

예수님의 그 사랑

-
High-er than the heav’ns a-bove,

하늘보다 더 높고

-
Deep-er than the depths of sea,

바다 보다 더 깊어

-
Last-ing as e-ter-ni-ty.

영원 무궁하도다

-
Love that found me wondrous tho’t!

나와 같은 죄인을

-
Found me when I sought Him not.

주가 찾아주셨네

- 3
Chief of sin-ners though I be,

나와 같은 죄인도

-
Christ is All-in-All to me.

예수님이 보살펴

-
All my wants to Him are known,

모든 염려 맡으사

-
All my sor-rows are His own.

항상 도와 주시네

-
He sus-tains the hid den life

예수 함께 계시며

-
Safe with Him from earth-ly strife.

참된 생명 얻겠네
