O-pen my eyes, that I may see

-

Tu ros-tro quie-ro ver, Je-sús;
진리를 보게 하소서

-
Place in my hands the won-der-ful key
Pon en mi co-ra-zón Tu bon-dad,

That shall un-clasp, and set me free.
Y da-me paz y san-ti-dad,
참빛을 찾게 하소서

-
Si-lent-ly now I wait for Thee,
Hu-mil-de-men-te_a-cu-do_a Ti,
깊으신 뜻을 알고자

-
Read-y, my God, Thy will to see.
Por-que tu tier-na voz o-í;
엎드려 기다리오니

-
O-pen my eyes, il-lu-mine me,
Mi guí-a sé, Es-pí-ri-tu
내 눈을 뜨게 하소서

-
Spir-it di-vine!
Con-so-la-dor.
성령이여
