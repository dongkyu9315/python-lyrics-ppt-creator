- 1
It came up-on the mid-night clear,
A me-dia no-che re-so-nó
그 맑고 환한 밤중에

That glo-rious song of old,
Glo-rio-so y sin i-gual

Un can-to an-gé-li-co de a-mor

-
To touch their harps of gold;
Su-bli-me y di-vi-nal;

"Peace on the earth, good-will to men.
Bri-llan-te luz res-plan-de-ció

From heaven’s all-gra-cious king."

La voz an-ge-li-cal.
