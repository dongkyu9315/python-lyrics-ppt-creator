383 Faith of Our Fathers
225 Omnipotente Padre Dios
383 환난과 핍박 중에도

- 1
Faith of our fa-thers! liv-ing still
Om-ni-po-ten-te Pa-dre Dios,
환난과 핍박 중에도

-
In spite of dun-geon, fire and sword;
Da-nos la fe del Sal-va-dor,
성도는 신앙 지켰네

-
O how our hearts beat high with joy
Que a nues-tros pa-dres fue sos-tén
이 신앙 생각할 때에

-
When-e’er we hear God’s glo-rious Word:
En los mo-men-tos de do-lor.
기쁨이 충만하도다

-
Faith of our fa-thers, ho-ly faith!
¡Has-ta la muer-te, en Cris-to es-tén
성도의 신앙 따라서

-
We will be true to Thee till death.
Nues-tra es-pe-ran-za y nues-tra fe!
죽도록 충성하겠네

- 2
Our fa-thers, chain-ed in pris-ons dark,
Da-nos la fe que dio po-der
옥중에 매인 성도나

-
Were still in heart and con-science free;
A los sol-da-dos de la cruz,
양심은 자유 얻었네

-
And blest would be their chil-dren’s fate
Que en cum-pli-mien-to del de-ber
우리도 고난 받으면

-
If they, like them, should die for Thee:
Die-ron sus vi-das por Je-sús.
죽어도 영광 되도다

-
Faith of our fa-thers, ho-ly faith!
¡Has-ta la muer-te, en Cris-to es-tén
성도의 신앙 따라서

-
We will be true to Thee till death.
Nues-tra es-pe-ran-za y nues-tra fe!
죽도록 충성하겠네

- 3
Faith of our fa-thers! We will love
Da-nos la fe que dé va-lor
성도의 신앙 본받아

-
Both friend and foe in all our strife;
Pa-ra en-fren-tar-nos con el mal,
원수도 사랑하겠네

-
And preach thee, too, as love knows how
Y por pa-la-bra y por ac-ción
인자한 언어 행실로

-
By kind-ly words and vir-tuous life:
Buen tes-ti-mo-nio siem-pre dar.
이 신앙 전파하리라

-
Faith of our fa-thers, ho-ly faith!
¡Has-ta la muer-te, en Cris-to es-tén
성도의 신앙 따라서

-
We will be true to Thee till death. A-men.
Nues-tra es-pe-ran-za y nues-tra fe! A-mén.
