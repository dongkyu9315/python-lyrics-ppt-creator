401 Go Forward
334 Al Cielo Vamos Santos Adelante
401 천성을 향해 가는 성도들아

- 1
Go for-ward, saints, to-wards to gate of hea-ven,
Al cie-lo va-mos San-tos a-de-lan-te,
천성을 향해 가는 성도들아

-
Let woes and hin-dran-ces nev-er you dis-may.
An-te pro-ble-mas no os cons-ter-na-réis;
앞 길에 장애를 두려 말아라

-
Led by the Spi-rit by the Fath-er giv-en,
Os gui-a-rá el es-pí-ri-tu del Pa-dre,
성령이 너를 인도하시리니

-
How can you faint; and why this de-lay?
¿Có-mo po-dréis a-ho-ra des-ma-yar;
왜 지체를 하고 있느냐

-
For-ward still, for-ward still!
Vá-mo-nos, vá-mo-nos,
앞으로 앞으로

-
Storm-ing the heights for hea-ven's gate,
Vá-mo-nos to-dos al cie-lo,
천성을 향해 나가세

-
Eyes firm fix'd on Zi-on and Vic-to-ry!
Nues-tros o-jos en la vic-to-ria es-tán;
천성 문만 바라고 나가세

-
Ea-ger to share hea-ven's ec-sta-sy.
La puer-ta del cie-lo por mí es-tán.
문 앞에 기다려 서있네

- 2
Let mock who may the way that you are tak-ing,
Aun-que se rei-rá de tu ca-mi-no al-guien,
너 가는 길을 누가 비웃거든

-
Be we but wit-ness-es, con-fi-dent and true.
Sé fiel tes-ti-go y no ten-gas te-mor;
확실한 증거를 보여주어라

-
Un-til the Spi-rit new dis-cip-les mak-ing,
Les gui-a-rá pues el Es-pí-ri-tu San-to
성령이 친히 감화하여 주사

Y en-con-tra-rán tam-bién la ver-dad;

Till all shall join you in your heav'n ward go-ing,
Has-ta que to-dos al cie-lo a-com-pa-ñen,

Sheathe not your weap-on, nor let your war-fare cease.
Co-ge es-pa-da y no en-vai-na-rás;
