386 March on, O Soul, with Strength

386 힘차게 일어나

- 1
March on, O soul, with strength, Like those strong men of old

힘차게 일어나 용감히 싸워라

-
Who 'gainst en-thron-ed wrong Stood con-fi-dent and bold;

저 마귀 물리친 옛 성도들 같이

-
Who, thrust in prison or cast to flame, Still made their glo-ry in your name.

그 어떤 형벌 당해도 주 영광 드러내리라

-




- 2
The sons of fa-thers we By whom our faith is taught

옛 성도 걸어간 그 길을 따라서

-
To fear no ill, to fight The ho-ly fight they fought;

이 거룩한 싸움 늘 싸워 이기며

-
He-ro-ic war-riors, ne'er from Christ By an-y lure or guile en-ticed.

이 세상 유혹 물리쳐 내 주만 따라 가겠네

-




- 3
March on, O soul, with strength, As strong the bat-tle rolls;

힘차게 나가자 큰 싸움할 때에

-
'Gainst lies and lusts and wrongs, Let cour-age rule our souls;

주님의 강한 손 내 능력 되시면

-
In keen-est strife, Lord, may we stand, Up-held and strength-ened by your hand.

저 마귀 능히 물리쳐 늘 승리 하게 되리라

-




- 4
Not long the con-flict soon The ho-ly war shall cease;

주님만 의지해 힘차게 나가자

-
Faith's war-fare end-ed: won The home of end-less peace.

이 거룩한 싸움 곧 끝이 나리니

-
Look up! the vic-tor's crown at length! March on, O soul, march on with strength. A-men.
