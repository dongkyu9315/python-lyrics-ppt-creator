How to use this website

1. Create a lyrics text file

- Create an empty text file with extension .txt

2. Fill out the title section

- The first 4 lines are the titles of the hymn in the order of English, Spanish, Korean, and Chinese
- For each line, hymn number comes first, the title comes after, and a whitespace separates them
- If the hymn does not have a number, the line should start with a whitespace or a symbol (recommended to use '#')
- e.g. (for below input, '#' will be written for Chinese hymn number)

340 'Tis so Sweet to Trust in Jesus
206 ¡Oh Cuan Dulce Es Fiar en Cristo!
340 구주 예수 의지함이
# 依靠救主耶稣

- If a language is missing, you MUST leave the line as empty
- e.g. (for below input, spanish and chinese are missing)

340 'Tis so Sweet to Trust in Jesus

340 구주 예수 의지함이

3. Iteratively fill out the lyrics section

- A slide in the PPT consists of 2 sections
- Each section starts with a '-' symbol
- A first section of of a slide may or may not have a verse number after '-' symbol with a whitespace in between

- 1
'Tis so sweet to trust in Je-sus,
Oh, cuan dul-ce es fiar en Cris-to
구주 예수 의지함이
救主耶稣我依靠
-
Just to take Him at His Word;
Y en-tre-gar-se to-do a el
심히 기쁜 일일세
我很开心。

- If a language is missing, you MUST leave the line as empty
- e.g. (for below input, spanish and chinese are missing)

- 1
'Tis so sweet to trust in Je-sus,

구주 예수 의지함이

-
Just to take Him at His Word;

심히 기쁜 일일세
